Who We Are

Food Made with
Heart & Soul

Flavor Hub started in 2012 with one simple dream — to serve food that feels like home. Founded by Chef Ramesh Kumar, our kitchen combines traditional Indian recipes with modern culinary techniques.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients. We believe great food brings people together — and that belief is cooked into every single plate we serve.
